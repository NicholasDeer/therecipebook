Classic Waffles

Description

This waffle recipe is a must-try. Light, fluffly and comforting, just like they're supposed to be. These waffles remind me of my childhood winter breaks when my brother and I stayed with our grandparents. These are the type of waffles that make children sit patiently in utter anticipation. Would highly recommend, day or night. This recipe looks and tastes amazing!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, melted butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle tge batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately!
